Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 3, 2025

Welcome to nether AG. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when using our apps. Please read it carefully.

1. Data Collection

Our apps do not collect any personal data from you. We do not track, store, or share any information related to your use of our apps.

2. Data Usage

Since no data is collected, we do not use, share, or process any data from users. Our apps are designed to work entirely offline and do not interact with any external servers or services that could collect data.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services in our apps that could collect or share data. There are no advertisements, analytics, or other external integrations that involve data collection.

4. Email

If you email us for support or other feedback, the emails with email addresses will be retained for quality assurance purposes. The email addresses will be used only to reply to the concerns or suggestions raised and will never be used for any marketing purpose.

5. Security

As no data is collected, no sensitive information is stored or transmitted. Therefore, no security measures are necessary for the protection of personal data.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to periodically review this page to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@nether.ch.
